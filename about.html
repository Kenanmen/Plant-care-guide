About Smart Plant Care

Smart Plant Care is an innovative approach to sustainable and efficient plant management. By integrating technology like soil moisture sensors with traditional plant care practices, we aim to optimize water usage, improve plant health, and support global environmental goals.

Our project is inspired by the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), particularly:

  • Goal 6: Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all.
  • Goal 12: Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns.
  • Goal 15: Protect, restore, and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems.

The soil moisture sensor is a key tool in our system. It enables precise monitoring of soil conditions, ensuring plants receive adequate hydration while conserving water resources. Combined with automated irrigation systems, our solution helps address challenges in agriculture, gardening, and environmental research.

By adopting smart plant care solutions, we contribute to a greener future, enhancing productivity and sustainability in plant care and agriculture worldwide.
